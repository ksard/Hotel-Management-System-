Hotel Policy

1. Check-In and Check-Out

  • Check-In
    • Check-in time is 12 pm, and guests are required to present valid government-issued identification.
    • Early check-in requests are subject to room availability.
  • Check-Out
    • Check-out time is 11 am.
    • Late check-out requests are subject to availability and may incur additional charges.

2. Reservations

  • Booking
    • Reservations can be made through our official website, by phone, or through authorized third-party booking platforms.
    • A valid credit card is required to secure a reservation.

3. Room Policies

  • Occupancy
    • The maximum occupancy per room is 2.
    • Additional charges may apply for extra guests.
  • Smoking
    • Our hotel is a non-smoking property. Smoking is prohibited in all guest rooms and indoor public areas.
  • Pets
    • Pets are not allowed unless specified as a pet-friendly hotel.
    • Guests with service animals should notify the hotel in advance.

4. Amenities and Services

  • Wi-Fi
    • Complimentary Wi-Fi is available throughout the hotel.
  • Parking
    • Parking is available for guests at a fee.
    • Valet parking services may be offered.

5. Housekeeping

  • Cleaning Services
    • Daily housekeeping services are provided.
    • Linen and towel changes are scheduled once a day.
  • Room Maintenance
    • Report any maintenance issues promptly to the front desk.

6. Security and Safety

  • Security Measures
    • The hotel employs 24-hour security personnel and surveillance systems.
  • Emergency Procedures
    • Familiarize yourself with emergency exits and procedures posted in the room.
    • In case of an emergency, contact the front desk immediately.

7. Guest Conduct

    • Guests are expected to conduct themselves in a respectful manner and adhere to the hotel's code of conduct.
    • Any disruptive or illegal behavior may result in eviction without a refund.

8. Feedback and Concerns

    • Guests are encouraged to provide feedback to help us improve our services.
    • Report any concerns or issues to the front desk during your stay.

9. Payment

    • Payment is required upon check-in.
    • Credit card holds or pre-authorizations may be applied.

10. Miscellaneous

    • The hotel is not responsible for lost or stolen items.
    • Management reserves the right to refuse service to anyone.
**This policy serves as a general guideline. Specific details may vary based on the hotel's location, size, and brand standards. Ensure that the policy complies with local laws and regulations.**
